=======================================
Pod Hotels
=======================================

aka capsule hotel [1]
aka Cyberpunk coffin [2]

Capsule hotels provide cheap, basic overnight accommodation for guests who do
not require or who cannot afford larger, more expensive rooms offered by more
conventional hotels.
